Summary: Crawford & Howell's modified t-test and revised standardized difference test (rsdt)

Description: This archive contains functions to compare a single case to a limited set of controls:
- Crawford & Howell's modified t-test
  Crawford JR, Garthwaite PH.
  Neuropsychology 2005 May;19(3):318-31)
- Crawford & Howell's revised standardized difference test (rsdt)
  Crawford JR, Garthwaite PH.
  Neuropsychology 2005 May;19(3):318-31)

Dependencies:
- tinv.m, e.g. from http://lasp.colorado.edu/cism/CISM_DX/code/CISM_DX-0.50/required_packages/octave-forge/extra/NaN/tinv.m
- tcdf.m, e.g. from http://www.statsci.org/matlab/statbox.html
